Jaguar

There are alot of big cats in the world. They are the third largest cat after the tiger and the lion. Like the panther, jaguars are very well trained swimmers. They will usually make their home near lakes and rivers since they are known for swimming over large rivers. They eat almost anything and can take down almost all of their prey with ease. They hunt in the day and the night They can take down prey that are 3 or 4 times bigger than they are due to their very large and powerful jaws and teeth.
