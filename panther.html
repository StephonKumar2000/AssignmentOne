Panther

The black panther is actually a jaguar or couger that has a mutated fur and skin color that can be black. The name for the reason they are black is called melanism. There can also be an occurence of albinism where the fur of the panther would be white and not black and is a rare mutation. Black panthers have very good hearing, eyesight, and a very strong jaw. They are very smart cats. For instance in the dark they are very stealthy like attackers. You can't see them well or at all in the dark because of how dark their fur is.
